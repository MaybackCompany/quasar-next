<Callout variant="fivem" title="What an MLO actually is">
  An MLO is a custom map interior: meshes, textures, collisions, and metadata streamed to the client. Like clothing packs, every byte is downloaded per player on join, and broken collisions or missing LODs cause falls-through-floor, invisible walls, and frame drops.
</Callout>

## Add it and keep frames

<StepGroup>
  <Step num={1} title="Understand the cost" outcome="You know why map mods crash servers.">
    MLOs and map mods are heavy streamed assets. Stacking many, or one badly optimized one, blows up memory and join times and can crash clients with weaker hardware. Size and quality discipline is the whole job.
  </Step>

  <Step num={2} title="Add the MLO as a resource" outcome="The interior loads and you can walk into it.">
    Place it in resources, ensure it, restart, and travel to the location. Confirm the interior loads and you can enter it.
  </Step>

  <Step num={3} title="Check collisions and LODs" outcome="No falling through floors, no invisible walls, no pop-in mess.">
    Walk the interior: do you stand on floors, bump walls, and see detail load smoothly as you approach (LODs)? Missing collisions drop you through the world; missing LODs cause ugly pop-in or invisible geometry at distance.
  </Step>

  <Step num={4} title="Run an optimization and QA pass" outcome="The map performs under real player load.">
    Trim unused assets, keep texture resolutions sane, and avoid stacking overlapping map mods over the same area (they fight and crash). Test with several players near the location and watch client FPS and memory.

    <ExpectedOutput>
      Players enter the interior with intact collisions, smooth LOD loading, and no crashes or major FPS drops.
    </ExpectedOutput>
  </Step>
</StepGroup>

## Common mistakes

<FailureList
  items={[
    { symptom: "Players fall through the floor", fix: "The MLO's collision is missing or not loading. Confirm the collision files stream and the resource started cleanly." },
    { symptom: "Invisible walls or geometry pops in late", fix: "LOD issue. The asset's detail levels are missing or misconfigured; use a properly built MLO." },
    { symptom: "Clients crash near the area", fix: "Asset weight or two map mods fighting over the same location. Remove overlaps and trim/optimize the assets." },
    { symptom: "Join times spiked after adding maps", fix: "Streamed map assets download per player. Reduce size and texture resolution; do not stack map mods." },
  ]}
/>
