## Run a compliant store

<StepGroup>
  <Step num={1} title="Publish a clear refund policy" outcome="Buyers and you both know the terms up front.">
    State your refund window and conditions before purchase: digital goods are often non-refundable once delivered, but you decide and publish the policy. A written policy you can point to defuses most disputes.
  </Step>

  <Step num={2} title="Understand chargebacks" outcome="You know the difference between a refund and a chargeback.">
    A **refund** is you returning money voluntarily. A **chargeback** is the buyer's bank reversing it, which carries fees and can hurt your processor standing. Respond to disputes with your published policy and delivery evidence (the buyer received the product).
  </Step>

  <Step num={3} title="Keep records" outcome="You can prove delivery and terms in a dispute.">
    Keep order records, delivery confirmation, and your published terms. In a chargeback, evidence that the buyer accepted the terms and received the goods is what protects you.
  </Step>

  <Step num={4} title="Stay inside platform and ecosystem rules" outcome="Your store and server are not at risk from what you sell.">
    Follow Tebex's terms and Cfx.re's platform monetization guidance. Avoid pay-to-win and prohibited items. When unsure whether a perk is allowed, treat it as not allowed until you confirm.
  </Step>
</StepGroup>
