Key shortcuts

Shared by our mapping instructor — these will save you hours:

  • G — Move / Grab
  • R — Rotate
  • S — Scale
  • Tab — Toggle Edit/Object mode
  • Shift+D — Duplicate
  • Ctrl+Z — Undo
  • E — Extrude
  • F — Fill (create face from selection)

MLO making & shortcuts

Community-contributed shortcut list for MLO creation:

  • N — Toggle side panel (properties)
  • Shift+A — Add object menu
  • Ctrl+J — Join objects
  • P — Separate selection
  • Alt+Z — Toggle X-Ray mode (see through walls)
  • Shift+Space — Maximize viewport
