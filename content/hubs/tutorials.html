CFX Membership & Licensing

No dedicated forum tutorial exists for this — here's what you need
  • Cfx.re Portal — Manage your servers and license keys
  • Keymaster — Generate license keys for your server
  • CFX Patreon — Membership tiers for higher player slots

Why you need it: A free license key is required for any public server. CFX Patreon membership unlocks higher player slots (up to 2048) and removes server browser restrictions. Without it, servers are limited to 10-48 slots based on population.
